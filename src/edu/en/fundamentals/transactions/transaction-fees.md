Transaction fees serve several **crucial purposes**. One of the primary reasons for these fees is that blockchain networks like Bitcoin and Ethereum can process only a limited number of transactions per day. To prioritize transactions, users can offer higher fees, ensuring their transfers are processed more quickly.
