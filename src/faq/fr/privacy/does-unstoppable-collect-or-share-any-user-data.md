# Does Unstoppable collect or share any user data?

Unstoppable doesn't collect any data or use analytics tools that may expose any data about its users. The wallet is designed to ensure a high level of privacy for its users.

- user data always remains on the user's device.
- the wallet doesn't collect any data about users.
- the wallet doesn't share any data about users.
- there are no user accounts or databases keeping user data elsewhere.
